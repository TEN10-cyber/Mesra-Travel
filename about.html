Who We Are

Mesra Travel is a Kota Kinabalu-based travel agency passionate about showing the world the beauty of Malaysia — from the coral reefs of Semporna to the summit of Mount Kinabalu.

We believe travel should feel personal, warm, and effortless. Our team of local experts crafts every itinerary with care, ensuring each journey is as unique as the traveller.

Whether you're planning a solo adventure, a family holiday, or a corporate retreat — we're here to make it unforgettable.
